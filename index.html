Our Mission & Vision

Driven by compassion, fueled by commitment.

Empowering Through Collective Action

Shitala Raghuraj Gurukulam Trust is dedicated to creating positive social change by empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, women empowerment and environment. We believe in equality, compassion, and collective action to build a better future for all. By partnering with local organizations and volunteers, we address critical issues such as poverty, gender inequality, and environmental protection. Our mission is to inspire hope, promote human rights, and ensure every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity and purpose. Together, we strive to build a brighter, more inclusive, and sustainable future for everyone.

With the help of our dedicated volunteers and donors, we strive to create a world where compassion and equality guide human progress. Our goal is to build self-reliant communities and inspire others to contribute to the welfare of society. Together, we believe we can make a lasting difference.

Our Pillars of Support

We believe in holistic community development.

Education Support

Comprehensive, tuition-free schooling from Nursery to Class 12, ensuring a continuous academic journey for all students.

Women Empowerment

Skill development and awareness programs focused on fostering independence and dignity for women in rural areas.

Healthcare Aid

Facilitating basic health check-ups and hygiene awareness camps to improve community well-being.

Food & Relief

Providing essential nutritional support and immediate relief during times of crisis and natural calamities.
